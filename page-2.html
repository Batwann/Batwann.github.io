In the Hisuian Pokédex of Pokémon™ Legends Arceus, pokémon number 139, the ghost-dark type Spiritomb, is a tough pokémon to obtain, starting afetr you have unlocked the request 'Eerie Apparations in the Night', where from that point on you have to find 107 Spiritomb wisps scattered across Hisui. The maps below will show where they all are, starting with: Jubilife Village.

Obsidian Fieldlands,

Crimson Mirelands.

Cobalt Coastlands.

Corenet Highlands.

And the Alabaster Icelands.

After you collect all of the wisps, you will go to the Shrouded Ruins in the Crimson Mirelands at night, where there is a big rock that resembles the Odd Keystone. once there, Vessa will tell you to turn around, and once you look back again, there will only be the 108th Spiritomb wisp in her place. Upon collecting it, if you turn around, thaere will be a level 60 Spiritomb for players to attempt to capture. Once you have done so, you will be able to capture Spiritomb in Massive Mass Outbreaks.
